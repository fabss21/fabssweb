Ella Mai


  • Ella Mai Howell (born 3 November 1994) is an English R&B singer. Her musical career began at London's British and Irish Modern Music Institute in 2014, during which time she auditioned as part of a trio on the 11th season of The X Factor. In 2015, she uploaded a four-track solo EP of originals to SoundCloud titled Troubled, and was discovered on social media by American producer DJ Mustard and signed with his record label, 10 Summers Records.

    • On 26 April 2018, she released a music video for "Boo'd Up" after it started to gain popularity.[14] The song became her first top ten song in the US in the following month, deeming it her "breakthrough hit."[15] Rolling Stone wrote that the single is "one of the biggest singles by a breakout female R&B singer in the past 10 years."[16] The single peaked at number five on the US Billboard Hot 100,[17] and broke the record for the most weeks at number one of any song by a woman on the R&B/Hip-Hop Airplay chart.[18] On 14 August, she joined Bruno Mars on the 24K Magic World Tour after Cardi B dropped out.[19] On 3 August, she released the single "Trip", with an accompanied music video releasing on 18 September.[20] The song peaked at number 11 on the Billboard Hot 100. Ella Mai released her self-titled debut album, a 16-track set featuring "Boo'd Up", on 12 October 2018, with guest appearances from Chris Brown, John Legend, and H.E.R.[21] The album sold 69,000 album-equivalent units with 17,000 coming from pure sales.[22] On 22 October 2018, Mai announced her debut tour set to begin in January 2019.[23][24] In November, she made two guest appearances on JID's song "Tiiied", along with 6lack and on Meek Mill's song "24/7", on the albums Dicapiro 2 and Championships respectively.
